Extra & by-product production not on the target sheet

These are highlighted/selected pieces that were produced but don't match a shape+size on your target sheet — round melee from sawing, sample cuts, or off-spec shapes. Worth tracking as bonus yield.

ShapeSize (approx.)PiecesTotal wt (ct)Value
How this is calculated: "Done" counts rows in the production sheet that are highlighted yellow (the selected/executed cut plan). Each piece is matched to a target row by shape family and its W:/L: dimensions; where a shape has several target variants at the same size (e.g. baguette height options), the closest Height % is used as the tiebreaker. Rows that aren't highlighted are treated as alternative plans that weren't cut, and are ignored. If a matched count or shape looks off, it's worth a manual spot-check — production sheet layouts vary.
